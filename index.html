Colibri

Glucose Control

Colibri app helps people with diabetes to improve their health. To achieve this it provides an easy way to track, review and analyse glucose levels, carbs and insulin.

It also helps to identify the correct dose of insulin required to cover the meal based on the current glucose level.

Add glucose and carbs

See 2 hours glucose trend

After adding current glucose level and carbs the app will show you expected glucose level after two hours. Adjust the insuling dose to keep your glucose within normal range.

Range limits can be adjusted in settings.

Track glucose trends

See all glucose readings for today on one graph

The graph is the great way to understand what is going on with your glucose levels. Pay more attention when the numbers aren't purple. That means your glucose level are out of safe range. This condition can be dangerous for your health. Discuss with your doctor how you need to act when your glucose is out of desired range.

Keep tracking

Improve your diabetes control

It is always great to remember the last thing that you did. Easy access to information for recent days allows you to identify relations between your food and insulin dosages.

Personalize the app

Use ratios that suits you the best

Are default insulin dosages too high or too low for you? You can modify it in settings. Choose ratios, that will work better for you. We all different, but it's not a problem.

Analyze your progress

Have statistics handy for your doctor appointment

In the Statistics of the app, you'll get your approximate HbA1C result, based on your glucose readings for that month. You can compare last tree month to keep in mind how your diabetes is going. Also, here you can find your average consumptions per day to ask right prescription and discuss your diabetes situation with your doctor.
